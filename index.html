Biography

About Nate

Nate Deuel is a multi-instrumentalist, arranger, and educator based in Bozeman, Montana. Current projects include his Bozeman Symphony Orchestra debut in Gershwin's Rhapsody in Blue this May, The Music Man with Montana TheatreWorks this summer, and the world premiere of A River Runs Through It with Opera Montana in September.
